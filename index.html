Official Flag of Peru

Peru is a country full of history and diversity, known worldwide for its ancient Inca city of Machu Picchu and its fascinating culture. Peruvian cuisine has gained global recognition. Its geography ranges from the Pacific coast to the heights of the Andes and the Amazon jungle, offering incredible biodiversity. Cities like Cusco and Lima combine colonial history with living traditions. In addition, the Nazca Lines and the Sacred Valley are other highlights that reflect the country's rich cultural heritage. Peru is a blend of natural wonders, ancestral heritage and a growing modern influence.
